Stan Efferding's Famous "Mash"

A staple of pro powerlifter and bodybuilder, Stan Efferding, the "Mash" consists of rice, ground beef, vegetables, broth, and more.

  • White rice
  • Ground beef
  • Baby carrots
  • Spinach
  • Beef broth
  1. Brown the ground beef in an Instant Pot
  2. Add white rice, beef broth, spinach, and baby carrots to Instant Pot
  3. Pressure cook for 9 minutes
  4. Enjoy an incredible, healthy meal
